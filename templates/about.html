About FlickFinder

I wrote this app as my final project for Harvard University's CS50 (introduction to computer science) massive-open-online-course. The main idea behind it is to alleviate the awkward and (at times) frustrating situation of wanting to watch a movie with one or more people, but being unable to agree on what to watch. It uses a similar design to the swipe-right/swipe-left mechanic employed by some dating apps, but with a focus on picking a movie to watch (as opposed to finding a date).

I'm not sure what I'm going to do with this app, but for now I'm hosting a live demo of it as I continue to develop it, with the hope that others will find it useful.
